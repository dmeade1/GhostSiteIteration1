About Ghost Yachts

Miami Beach's Trusted Yacht Specialists

Located in the heart of Miami Beach, Ghost Yachts is your trusted team of yacht brokers and specialists. Whether you are looking to sell your existing yacht, purchase a new one, or build the yacht of your dreams, the Ghost Yachts team is here to assist you every step of the way.

We proudly offer an impressive lineup of new and used yachts including an exclusive US partnership with Astondoa. At Ghost Yachts, we combine our passion for yachting with unparalleled customer service to ensure that your boating dreams become a reality.
